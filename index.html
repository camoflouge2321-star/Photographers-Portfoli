About Me

A Photographer
with a Passionate Eye

Hello, I'm Luca. I believe every photograph is a portal — a window to a feeling, a story, a fleeting second in time, preserved forever. With over a decade behind the lens, I've had the privilege of capturing life's most extraordinary moments across the world.

From intimate wedding ceremonies in Tuscany to raw street scenes in Tokyo, my work is driven by an insatiable curiosity for the human experience and the beauty that surrounds us.
